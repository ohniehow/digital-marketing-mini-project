Expat Life

The population of Dubai is roughly 2 million and rising. The Emirati citizens are in the minority both in Dubai and the UAE as the majority of people are expatriates from all over the world. The UAE is a largely tax free country, which is one of the top reasons why people from all over the world decide to move to UAE and work in Dubai or Abu Dhabi.

Lifestyle

Dubai Lifestyle

Dubai has a vibrant nightlife scene with everything from grungy live music style bars, to some of the finest restaurants and chefs from around the world. If you make the most of your time working and living in Dubai and get out & about, you will soon find that you have friends from all over the world and many incredible stories to share about expat life in Dubai.

Life in Dubai

Working and Living in Dubai

Your accommodation will depend on how much money you have, hence depends on what salary you earn with your work in Dubai. Popular places to find accommodation are Facebook Forums, Dubizzle and PropertyFinder. Also, working and living in Dubai goes almost hand in hand as it is wise to pick your accomodation based on the location of your work in Dubai.
